\noindent This supplement contains the curated panel rationale, complete instrument-availability matrix, all primary and exploratory MR estimates, TYK2 validation, colocalization, full delta-method sensitivity results, and the raw-data reproduction audit. Machine-readable versions of all tables accompany the submission.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent Instrument-availability estimates are conditional on the GTEx significant-pair files, which do not contain complete all-pairs cis summary statistics. Primary and relaxed-threshold results are kept as separate hypothesis families throughout. LD-dependent multi-SNP analyses, Steiger directionality, reverse MR, pQTL-null claims, and formal power percentages are not included because the validated local inputs did not support those analyses.
